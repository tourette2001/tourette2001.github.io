HISTORY OF NIGHTRIDE CYCLING CLUB

THE CLUB WAS BORN FROM A SHARED CYCLING HOBBY OF A FEW FRIENDS FROM HELSINKI IN 2019.

FOR THE FIRST FEW YEARS, WE RODE EVERY THURSDAY, REGARDLESS OF THE TIME OF YEAR OR THE AMOUNT OF SNOW.

CURRENTLY, THE CLUB HAS 72 MEMBERS, AND THE NUMBER IS CONSTANTLY GROWING, WHICH SPEAKS TO THE APPEAL OF THE COMMUNITY AND THE INCREASING POPULARITY OF CYCLING IN THE HELSINKI METROPOLITAN AREA.

OUR ACTIVITY HAS EXPANDED FROM MERE THURSDAY RIDES TO WEEKLY RIDES WITH VARIOUS THEMES, EVENTS, AND JOINT WEEKEND TRIPS.

WE ARE COMMITTED TO CREATING A RELAXED AND SUPPORTIVE ATMOSPHERE WHERE EVERY CYCLIST IS WELCOME, REGARDLESS OF THEIR BACKGROUND. THIS INCLUDES UPHOLDING THE PRINCIPLES OF A SAFE SPACE.
